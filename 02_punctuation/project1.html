Utilizing a public domain text "Punctuation Marks" by Theodor W. Adorno, the following studies and outputs explore the functionality of the punctuation marks in two different ways: 1, by isolating the punctuation marks from its context (texts) and 2, by pronouncing the punctuation marks out loud instead of the text. The texts are republished through online platforms and the way in which they are published correspond to the qualities or characteristics of the specific platform.
